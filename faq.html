Frequently Asked Questions

About GEMERA

Who is behind GEMERA Project?

A group of entrepreneurs with knowledge and passion for the blockchain technology and the cryptocurrency ecosystem. Meet our team.

What is GEMERA Project?

It is a global project which issues crypto-tokens backed by Colombian emeralds, which are insured and stored in a security vault in Hong Kong.

For more information, read GEMERA’s White Paper.

Which is GEMERA Project´s official communication channel?

Our official communication channel will be Twitter (@GemeraProject)

What are GEMERA tokens?

GEMERA is a crypto-token backed by Colombian emeralds. It can be used as a store of value, be transferred among digital wallets, converted into other crypto-tokens, cryptocurrencies or fiat currencies, or redeemed for physical emeralds.

What is GEMA?

It is the ticker symbol that represents the GEMERA tokens.

GEMERA Tokens

How are GEMERA tokens created?

GEMERA tokens are created through an Ethereum smart contract each time a batch of emeralds is stored in a safety vault in Hong Kong and uploaded into the marketplace; afterwards, a token distribution’s period is available for investors.

What is a token distribution?

It is the period of time when the GEMERA Project issues GEMERA tokens to be acquired by investors.

How often does a token distribution occur?

A token distribution occurs every time a significant batch of emeralds is stored in a safety vault in Hong Kong and uploaded into the marketplace.

How can I acquire GEMERAs in a token distribution period?

The GEMERA tokens have to be acquired with Ether (ETH), the Ethereum cryptocurrency, following the instructions in www.gemera.io

What is the bonus granted to investors in the token distribution periods?

During the first token distribution period, there will be a 10% bonus for investors. The bonus of the other token distribution periods will be determined previously and informed with enough anticipation.

What is the minimum amount I can invest in a GEMERA token distribution?

0,001 ethers.

Are there network fees when acquiring GEMERA Tokens?

Yes. It corresponds to the ‘gas consumption’ of the Ethereum network. This fee is dynamic and generally low.

How can I acquire GEMERAs when a token distribution is not available?

GEMERAs can be acquired in compatible exchanges. Although, this option is not available in the early stage of the GEMERA Project, and its success depends on the integration with exchanges.

Can I send Ether to a token distribution ETH address from an exchange?

No. Do not send Ether from an exchange to participate in a token distribution. If you send Ether to the GEMERA Token Distribution Contract address from an exchange account, your GEMERA tokens will be allocated to the exchange's Ethereum account, and you may never receive or be able to recover your GEMERA tokens.

How does the GEMERA token distribution work?

Every time a significant batch of emeralds is stored in a security vault in Hong Kong, a token distribution to acquire GEMERAs is open during a period of time. To be part of the token distribution, you just have to follow the instructions in www.gemera.io

How can GEMERA tokens be used?

  • GEMERAs can be converted into fiat or cryptocurrencies in exchanges (this option is not available in the early stage of the GEMERA Project, and its success depends on the integration with exchanges).
  • GEMERAs can be transferred instantly to other compatible wallets.
  • GEMERAs can be redeemed for physical emeralds. - token distribution

Technology

What is a smart contract?

They are contracts whose terms, agreements and conditions between two parties are written in code, which is hosted in a decentralized blockchain system, without the need of an intermediary. Thanks to smart contracts, transactions are traceable, transparent and irreversible.

What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is one of the most used, established, safe and proven decentralized platforms for smart contracts, which uses blockchain technology.

What happens if I lose the private keys of my compatible wallet?

GEMERA Project does not hold the private keys. They are allocated in GEMERA holder’s compatible digital wallet, who must make sure to keep the keys in a secure place. The loss of the private keys will cause the holder not to be capable of having access to the GEMERAS or making any transactions with them. In such case GEMERA Project will not be able to recover the tokens

What wallets can I use to send Ether to acquire GEMERAs in a token distribution’s period?

GEMERA Project has tested Metamask and Ethereum Wallet, and they work properly; so, it is recommended to use these wallets to acquire GEMERA tokens in a token distribution’s period.

To be compatible, a wallet must meet the following criteria:

  • Able to export your private key.
  • Able to call arbitrary contract methods.

What wallets are incompatible to acquire GEMERAs in a token distribution’s period?

Do not use an incompatible wallet.

There are many incompatible wallets. Please check to make sure that your wallet meets the compatible wallet criteria before sending any Ether. The following wallets are known to be incompatible wallets but this is not a complete list. Do not use any of the following wallets to participate in the GEMERA token distribution:

Any Bitcoin exchange, Any Ethereum exchange, Bitfinex, Bittrex, Bitstamp, Cex.io, Coinbase, Exodus, Jaxx, Kraken, Poloniex.

Redemption

How to redeem GEMERAs for physical emeralds?

The redemption of GEMERAs for emeralds will be available once a year, and it is composed of the following steps:

  • Selection: The GEMERA holder selects the emerald/s in the marketplace and transfers the value in GEMERAs to a specified address.
  • Transaction: Once the GEMERA transfer is successful, the order is processed.
  • Delivery: The emerald/s can be delivered to the investor's country of residence by secured mail or can be collected directly in Hong Kong.

How often can I redeem GEMERAs for physical emeralds?

Once a year, in a redemption period whose date will be notified with enough anticipation.

What is the marketplace?

It is an e-commerce (market.gemera.io) with the information of each one of the emeralds stored in a safety vault of a safe deposit box company in Hong Kong and ready to be redeemed with GEMERAs.

The information of the emeralds includes name, characteristics, price in GEMERAs, and certification, as well as a 360° video, and photographs from different angles.

How is the value in GEMERAs of the emeralds determined?

GEMERA has developed a model along with gemologists and experts in jewelry and gemstones, which considers color, clarity, carat weight and cut to determine the quality of the emeralds and set up the price. Please, visit the GEMERA Setting Value Process, in GEMERA white paper (item 5).

Can I collect the emeralds directly in Hong Kong?

GEMERA Project will place a special office in Hong Kong where GEMERA holders who want to redeem their tokens can recollect their emeralds, during the redemption process, which is available once a year.

Why choosing Colombian emeralds to back the GEMERA tokens?

Colombian emeralds are the most desired emeralds in the market, due to their unique characteristics; so it is a valuable asset to back GEMERA tokens.

My GEMERA tokens are directly linked to a specific emerald?

No. Whenever the GEMERA holders want to redeem their tokens for emeralds, they can visit the marketplace (market.gemera.io) to find the gemstone/s they want to acquire according to the number of GEMERAs they would like to redeem.

Why the emeralds are stored in a security vault in Hong Kong?

Because Hong Kong is one of the major centers for the trade of emeralds, and has the appropriate infrastructure to save this type of assets.

How do the storage of emeralds and admin fees work?

A yearly fee is assigned to cover the costs of storage and insurance of emeralds, operational, and administrative processes, as well as legal affairs. This fee is obtained through the release of a number of new tokens every year. To keep the consistency between the blockchain and the marketplace, the emerald value of each GEMERA will be increased up to 2% per year in the marketplace. This process is audited and public.

Is there a contingency fund?

In the case of the emeralds, these are insured in the event of loss, theft, accident, or damage. Additionally the GEMERA Project has a fund to cover other contingencies, such as anomalies present in the gemstones during the audit process or to cover a legal litigation.

How can I be sure the emeralds are stored in Hong Kong?

The GEMERA Project focuses on the transparency of the process throughout its value chain: the selection of the emeralds, the certification every stone must have, the storage in Hong Kong, how the emerald’s quality is determined, how to give them a price in GEMERA tokens, and how the holders can redeem them, among other aspects of equal importance.

Therefore, each emerald in Hong Kong is exhibited in GEMERA Project’s marketplace (market.gemera.io), with the name of the safe deposit box company where it is stored, and an audit process will take place to guarantee the existence, origin and storage of the emeralds.
