INSTRUCTIONS

1. Metamask

Add the Metamask Wallet Extension in Google Chrome.


2. Add GEMERA Token Contract Address

Copy the GEMERA Token Contract address:

0xe9a2090557e9666676168cDE58C8e18C5A03b2b7

(Please don't send Ether to this address)

Inside the TOKENS Tab, click in the ADD TOKEN button and paste the Token Contract Address.



Note: Check the Token Symbol field shows the word GEMA and the Decimals of Precision field is 18.


3. Token Validation

Validate that the token was added correctly and click on SEND.




4. Transaction

Copy the GEMERA Distribution Address:

0x3159D7777a38D092B1Cb1eF74Cce63fD0dD67b97

Paste the GEMERA Distribution Address on the Recipient Address field, set the amount to transfer in Ether (ETH), and click on NEXT:




Note: The amount you will receive in GEMERAs is the equivalent of the rate of Ether plus the bonus.

5. Confirmation

Confirm the transaction clicking on SUBMIT.



Confirm that you received the GEMERA Tokens (GEMA) in your Wallet (usually it takes less than five minutes).
